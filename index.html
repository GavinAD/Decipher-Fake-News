Don’t Be Fooled: Deciphering Fake News

True or False? That’s a good question!

What is really true? In the digital age, more information than any person could ever
learn is available at your fingertips. But with this much information, some of it will
inevitably be false. This begs the question, what information should we believe? Especially
when around every corner there’s information to support your own biases. Let’s see how well you do.
